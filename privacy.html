Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-11

This privacy policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you visit this site.

Information we collect

Server logs. Our hosting provider collects standard request logs (IP, user agent, referrer) for security and performance.

Email correspondence. If you email us, we keep that email so we can respond.

We do not set tracking cookies, run third-party advertising pixels, or sell your data.

Affiliate links

Most links on this site direct you to third-party hotel booking platforms (e.g., Hilton, Marriott, IHG, Hyatt, Choice, Best Western, Wyndham, Hotels.com, Expedia, Priceline, Booking.com) through affiliate programs. Those platforms may set their own cookies once you arrive on their site. Their privacy policies govern your interaction with them.

Third-party services

Hosting: GitHub Pages. Email: standard email providers. We do not use Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, or other ad-network trackers.

Your rights

If you're in the EU, UK, or California, you have the right to request what data we hold about you and to ask us to delete it. Email privacy@austinlodging.com.

Children

This site is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes

If we update this policy we'll change the "Last updated" date above.
