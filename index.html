Austin Hotels — Common Questions

The things travelers ask most before booking in Austin.

How far is Austin-Bergstrom Airport (AUS) from downtown?

Austin-Bergstrom International is about 7 miles southeast of downtown — roughly a 15–20 minute drive (longer at rush hour or during big events). Most downtown, Rainey Street, and South Congress hotels are within that window.

Where should I stay for SXSW or ACL Festival?

For SXSW, stay downtown — most venues, panels, and showcases are within walking distance and traffic is brutal. For ACL Fest (held in Zilker Park), look at Zilker, South Lamar, Barton Springs, or downtown. Either way, book months ahead — rates run three to five times normal and the best hotels sell out early.

Is 6th Street a good area to stay in Austin?

"Dirty Sixth" — the east end of 6th Street downtown — is the rowdy college-bar strip: fun if that's your scene, loud if it isn't. For nightlife that's still walkable but more grown-up, Rainey Street, the 2nd Street District, or South Congress are better bases.

Do I need a car in Austin?

Downtown, Rainey Street, and South Congress are walkable and rideshare-friendly, so for a short city trip you can skip the car. To reach the Hill Country, Lake Travis, the Domain, the Circuit of the Americas, or anywhere in the suburbs, you'll want one — Austin has very limited rail transit.

When is the cheapest time to book a hotel in Austin?

Rates in Austin are usually lowest about three to four weeks out, and mid-week check-ins (Tuesday and Wednesday) tend to beat weekends. Prices spike during major conventions and events, so check the city's event calendar before locking in a date.

Which area of Austin should I stay in?

It depends on your trip — Downtown / 2nd Street District, South Congress (SoCo), Rainey Street are among the most popular places to base yourself. See our neighborhood guide for what each area is known for and where to stay in each.

Do Austin hotels charge resort fees or parking fees?

Some do. Resort fees ($25–45/night) and parking ($20–60/night at city-center hotels) are often not included in the headline rate, so check the full price breakdown before booking. Many hotels waive parking for loyalty members.
